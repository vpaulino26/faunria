# Architects

The ancient architects responsible for most of Faunria’s advances. An extinct species now, but their impact is seen every day.

The architects are beaverfolk. All the beaverfolk are extinct.

When the Architects lived in Meakcreak, it’s name was Beavcreak. The modern name reflects the sorry state the Creak ended up in. 

Instead of dragons, beavers learn to control trees. They could make trees punch people with high velocity and kill them or smash them or bash them about with ease, which led to the FaunFlora war
