# The Riverbends

# Main Inhabitants

Birds, Rats, Otters, Frogs

# Geography

**River-forest floodplain with marshy backwaters.**  

A wooded riparian corridor supports birds in the trees, rats in bankside burrows and debris tangles, otters in the river and oxbows, and frogs in wetlands/ponds.

# Capital

**Crownbow:** a fortified town on a high natural levee where the main river loops around an oxbow lake. Otter canals cut through the settlement, frog reed-gardens act as nurseries, and rat-built boardwalk districts sprawl over the marsh.

## Capital Design

### Descriptive Features

- There is a main river that surrounds the city
- the overall city kinda looks like a crown with the way the bordering river outlines the city
- Canals were created as means of travel throughout the city (think Venice)
- There is an oxbow lake disonnected from the main river where frogs live (steady water, no river flow)
- Otters live on the coast and uses the river to navigate around town
- Rats made boardwalks to cross the river, live on the surface
- Birds live in the few trees in the city but also live on the surface
