# Otterfolk

traits taken from [https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/Otterfolk_(5e_Race)](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/Otterfolk_(5e_Race))

# Personality

Otterfolk are playful, adrenaline-seeking gamesters and storm chasers. Inveterate water dwellers, otterfolk are gregarious and sociable, with a taste for excitement that can drive them to reckless, daredevil antics that other animalfolk see as thoughtless and unnecessary. At their best, otterfolk are daring, jovial, and adventurous. At their worst, otterfolk are careless, frivolous, and prone to putting others in harm’s way.

# **Otterfolk Traits**

### Ability Score Increase

Your [Strength](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Strength) and [Intelligence](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Intelligence) scores both increase by 1.

### Age

A typical otterfolk reaches maturity at two years old, and can live to a maximum of 60 years.

### Alignment

Otterfolk tend to be neutral, though it is not uncommon for them to be lawful or chaotic.

### Size

A typical otterfolk is between 2 and 3 feet in height. Your size is Small.

### Speed

Your base walking speed is 25 feet, and you have a swim speed of 30 feet.

### Darkvision

You can see in [dim light](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Dim_Light) within 60 feet of you as if it were [bright light](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Bright_Light), and in [darkness](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Vision_and_Light) as if it were [dim light](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Dim_Light). You can't discern color in [darkness](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Vision_and_Light), only shades of gray.

### Otter Resilience

You have [advantage](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Advantage) on [Constitution](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Constitution) [saving throws](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Saving_Throws) against magic and against being [poisoned](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Conditions#Poisoned).

### Hold Breath

You can [hold your breath](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Suffocating) for up to an hour.

### Languages

You can speak Common and Otterfolk.

# Subraces

You must choose between three subraces of otterfolk: Builder, Fighter, or Fisher.

## **Builder**

### Ability Score Increase

Your [Constitution](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Constitution) score increases by 1.

### Hard Worker

You have advantage on [Constitution](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Constitution) [saving throws](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Saving_Throws) against [exhaustion](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Conditions#Exhaustion).

### Natural Artisan

You are proficient in one [artisan's tool](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Tools) of your choice.

---

## **Fighter**

### Ability Score Increase

Your [Strength](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Strength) score increases by 1.

### Claws

You can choose for your [unarmed strikes](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Unarmed_Strike) deal slashing damage equal to 1d4 + your [Strength](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Strength) modifier, instead of the bludgeoning damage normal for an unarmed strike.

### Natural Athlete

You are proficient in the [Athletics](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Athletics_Skill) skill.

---

## **Fisher**

### Ability Score Increase

Your [Dexterity](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Dexterity) score increases by 1.

### Fast Swimmer

Your swim speed increases by 10 feet.

### Survivor's Instinct

You have [advantage](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Advantage) on [Wisdom](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Wisdom) ([Survival](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Survival_Skill)) checks made in naturally-occurring rivers, streams, and lakes.

# Tips & Recommendations

## Name Examples

Otterfolk names tend to come from things in nature, such as Rock, Thistle, and Bark.

Examples: Bark, Oak, Rock, Spruce, Thistle, Brook, Rosa, Ivy, Moss, Petal, Pine, Leaf

## Class Recommendations

Wizard, Rogue, Sorcerer
