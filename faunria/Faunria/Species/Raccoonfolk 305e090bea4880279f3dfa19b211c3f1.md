traits taken from [https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/Raccoon_Folk_(5e_Race)](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/Raccoon_Folk_(5e_Race))

# Personality

The raccoonfolk of Faunria are hoarders, collectors, and wanderers who are just as much at home on the road and in the wild expanse as they are in large villages. Their journeys shape their free-spirited attitudes and love of nature. You'll seldom ﬁnd a raccoonfolk who would turn down an adventure, no matter the dangers ahead. Other species might see them as ﬂakey, willing to shirk responsibility in favor of excitement, but to raccoonfolk there is no obligation more important than self-discovery. At their best, raccoonfolk are strong-willed, adventurous, and entertaining. At their worst, they are scatterbrained, solitary, and impulsive.

# **Raccoonfolk Traits**

### Ability Score Increase

Your [Dexterity](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Dexterity) score increases by 2.

### Age

Raccoon Folk reach maturity around 15 and live to be around 80. The oldest ever recorded is 100 years old.

### Alignment

Raccoon Folk do not really pick a side and wish to remain neutral, but others lean more toward a chaotic path.

### Size

Raccoon Folk are mostly small, from 3 feet to 4 feet, the tallest being 4'5". Your size is Small.

### Speed

Your base walking speed is 30 feet. Your base climbing speed is 25 feet.

### Darkvision

You can see in [dim light](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Dim_Light) within 60 feet of you as if it were [bright light](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Bright_Light), and in [darkness](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Vision_and_Light) as if it were [dim light](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Dim_Light). You can't discern color in [darkness](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Vision_and_Light), only shades of gray.

### Sleuthing Skills

You gain [proficiency](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Proficiency_Bonus) on any [Investigation](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Investigation_Skill) rolls when looting bodies or piles of junk. If you already have proficiency, you instead gain [advantage](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Advantage) on the check.

### Sticky Fingers

Living up to your race's reputation, you gain [proficiency](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Proficiency_Bonus) in the [Sleight of Hand](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Sleight_of_Hand_Skill) skill.

### Light as a Feather

You take no damage from falling when you fall from 35 feet or lower.

### Clever Girl

You gain [advantage](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Advantage) on [Survival](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Survival_Skill) skill checks.

### Languages

You can speak, read, and write Common and Raccoon.

# Subraces

## **Tanuki**

### Ability Score Increase

Your [Charisma](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Charisma) score increases by 1.

### The Spook

You gain [advantage](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Advantage) on [Stealth](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Stealth_Skill) checks.

### Belly Music

You have the ability to expand your stomach large enough for it to be played like drums. This can be used just like any bard instrument.

---

## **Opossum**

### Ability Score Increase

Your [Wisdom](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Wisdom) score increases by 1.

### Brush it Off

You have resistance to poison damage and have [advantage](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Advantage) on all [Medicine](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Medicine_Skill) checks that include diseases.

### Social Creatures

You gain [advantage](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Advantage) on all [Charisma](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Charisma) ([Persuasion](https://www.dandwiki.com/wiki/5e_SRD:Persuasion_Skill)) checks with humanoids.

# Tips & Recommendations

## Name Examples

Raccoons typically name their children after food or some type of nature element.

Examples: Chi'ken, Shroomboom, Char, Broth, Rigby, Spinach, Rivermo, Milco

## Class Recommendations

Bard, Druid, Rogue
