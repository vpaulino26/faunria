# Story

# #1 - Beginning

Sweat. That’s all to be felt in the heat of Summer in the South. Hot as it drips, but the shades of Logjam trees cools the sweat fine enough to bare. Only today the sweat would continue to grow in heat while increasing flow state as it faucets down the body. The next thing felt is that rumble in the your belly. The harsh current of “The Wash” (river) twists the stomachs of those ill-fit to sail or swim through them. If it spills your bowels (shit your pants), you’re meant to die at sea. That’s what the people of Logjam say anyway, however few of them. No matter, The infamous whitewater river is the quickest route to reach the Pools. 

SCENE NOTES:

- they’re on a prison boat to the Pools from Crownbow, the capital of Faunria, located in the Riverbends. The King of Faunria needs to deliver this note to the Lord of Pools.

- after exposition, they encounter a swimming being that’s very tan, almost orange, mayhaps a little red. It does not speak, it does not negotiate. They fight it maybe or maybe not. If they kill it, it comes back to life. Once it gets close, it will explode on them. If all senses are removed from the brain, then it explodes. Then they get to the Pools.
